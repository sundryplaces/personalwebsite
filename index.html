In sundry places

A digital garden comprised of thoughts, sounds, and snapshots from a blessed life in troubled times.

Professionally I’m a junior high science, math, and career and technology teacher. I work in alternative education and my assignment includes managing the school makerspace and facilitating youth social innovation labs for other schools. I explore these things in my garden, but, in general, I'm the work-to-live type.

When I’m living, I’m making/fixing stuff, creating sounds, enjoying the outdoors, connecting with my people, trying to find God's light in everything, eating good vegetarian food, and enjoying simple domesticity with my beautiful partner.

This is a garden, not a polished blog. It's written for me, but you're welcome to see. Everything found here is a fraction of baked, open to discussion, and subject to growth.

Here are some of the topics I’m interested in:

  • Music / sound
  • Making
  • Repairing
  • Faith
  • Nature
  • Post-humanism
  • Social innovation
  • Pedagogy
